Personal Hygiene

Here are some things YOU can do to maintain overall personal hygiene:

  • Avoid touching your face with your hands
  • Wash your hands frequently

    • See a doctor if you feel unwell

    Other Precautions to Take

    We should continue to remain vigilant and play our part as reponsible Singaporeans to lower the number of cases in Singapore
  • Wear a mask at all times unless doing strenous exercise
  • Observe a social distance of at least 1m
  • Limit social gatherings. Meet online where possible
  • Avoid crowded areas by checking spaceout.gov.sg
  • Stay up-to-date on the latest news about the COVID-19 situation at www.gov.sg/features/covid-19
